/ screens.json. The required-coverage union is COMPUTED FROM EACH PROJECT — there
is no project-specific config. Same script works on:
  - Hatch portfolio (this test) — 26 + 13 + 19 + 23 required
  - book-swap (when it runs) — different required-union per its components.md
  - finance-track (when it runs) — different required-union per its components.md
  - gotribe-* (when they run) — different per-project required-union
The mechanism is structural, not content-specific. Future projects automatically
benefit without any factory edit.
